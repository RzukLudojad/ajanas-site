01

Problem

Finding the right people to play with

Problem statement

Finding people to play together often proves to be hard.
There is a lackof a tool that would allow you to find people in a convenient way.

Value Proposition

A mobile networking application, that allows users to find other musicians nearby and arrange music jams and rehearsals.

MVP

A working prototype of mobile app with documented research and design process.

Benchmarks

Finding the right people to play with

  • Physical ads

    Target limited to people already attending the space, information important for users (see next slide) is often missing

  • Non-dedicated online tools

  • Dedicated web tools (ad boards)

    • Facebook: Very popular, groups centered around one city/locationis a big plus, but there’s a lack of proper filtering and in most ads important info is missing as well.
    • Ad boards: Consistently losing popularity, not really fit for musical ads. Only 1 out of 9 respondents said they’ve used them for finding musicians.

  • Mobile apps

    • Bandmate

    • Vampr

  • Other Inspirations
